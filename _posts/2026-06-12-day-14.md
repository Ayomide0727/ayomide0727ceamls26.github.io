  Program. During our research lab session, we focused on preparing for our
  weekly showcase presentation. We reviewed our accomplishments, challenges,
  and progress from the week and discussed our goals for the upcoming week.

  an opportunity to practice communicating our work clearly and receive feedback

  shared our plans for the next phase of the project.

  from other research groups. It was interesting to learn about the diverse
  projects being conducted across the program and see the different approaches
  teams are taking to solve problems using AI and machine learning.
