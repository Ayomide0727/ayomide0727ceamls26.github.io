  project as a whole, including the methodology, results, and conclusions.

  Today reminded me that taking a step back is sometimes just as important as
  constantly working. After weeks of learning new concepts, debugging code, and

  the collaborative nature of research. Reading the sections written by my
  teammates helped me better understand the complete story of our project and
  how each person's contributions fit together. It also prepared me to discuss
  any part of our research with confidence during our final presentations.

  As we move toward the final weeks of the internship, I am excited to polish
  the remaining deliverables and reflect on how much I have grown as both a
  researcher and a software developer throughout this experience.
