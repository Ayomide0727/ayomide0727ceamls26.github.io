what_i_learned: |
  Today was a different but exciting day for our team. We spent most of the day
  filming our 90-second project pitch video for DermaBridge. Before recording,
  we planned our script, assigned speaking roles, and practiced our delivery to
  make sure we could clearly communicate our project within the time limit.

  It was interesting to see how much thought goes into creating a short pitch.
  Even though we have spent weeks working on the technical aspects of our
  project, condensing everything into just 90 seconds required us to focus on
  the most important parts of our research and explain them in a way that anyone
  could understand.

blockers: |
  We did not encounter any major technical challenges today. The main challenge
  was coordinating the filming process, making sure everyone was comfortable
  with their speaking parts, and getting good takes for each scene.

reflection: |
  Today was one of the most enjoyable days of the internship. It was fun working
  with my teammates to create a more dramatic and engaging video rather than
  giving a traditional presentation. The experience showed me that presenting
  research can be both informative and creative.

  Tomorrow, we plan to finish editing the video and submit our final pitch. I am
  excited to see the finished product and share our work with others.
