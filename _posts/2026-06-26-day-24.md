  Today was dedicated to preparing for our Mid-Summer Research Presentation.
  The nine research teams were divided into three groups, and each team
  presented their project to one another as a practice session before the
  official presentation on Monday.

  My team presented our research progress, including our accomplishments,
  challenges, and current results. After our presentation, we received
  constructive feedback from Elijah, one of the program coordinators. His
  suggestions helped us identify areas where we could improve the clarity,
  organization, and overall flow of our presentation.

  We also had the opportunity to watch other teams present their projects. It
  was interesting to see the different research topics and presentation styles,
  and I picked up a few ideas that could help strengthen our own presentation.

  I did not encounter any major technical challenges today. Our main focus was
  refining our presentation and ensuring that our research story was easy for
  the audience to follow.

  communicate our research.

  Watching the other teams present also gave me new ideas and helped me feel
  more prepared for the Mid-Summer Presentation. I am looking forward to making
  the suggested improvements over the weekend and presenting our work on Monday.
